The coastal emblem delicacy

Meet the most eaten food in the coastal of the three east african countries .

Kenya,Uganda and somalia

ingrendents

  • Pilau masala
  • curry powder
  • sack of rice
  • beef
  • salt
  • potatoes
  • shawarma

Steps

  1. Boil 100 litres of water in a sufuria
  2. Put the rice in the water after it has attained some hot degrees
  3. Mix the availabvle ingrendents in a separate pan
  4. Add the curry powder to enhance taste
  5. Boil meat in a separate sufuria
  6. After they are all good mix the meat and the cooked rice
  7. Serve the emblem delicacy and enjoy the meal
