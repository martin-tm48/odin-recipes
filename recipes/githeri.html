The absolute foodmount

This is the type of food that rules the streets of kenya. It is consumed by more than 10 million people and its easy to prepare.

ingrendents

  • Beans
  • Maize
  • onions
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Put clean beans and maize in a hot boiling water
  2. powder some cayenne pepper to the mixture
  3. When the beans are soft transfer them to a new sufuria
  4. Then cut onions into small sizes and add some cooking oil
  5. Put one spoonfull of salt and stir thouroughly
  6. When soft,serve the dish when hot for a tasty githeri
